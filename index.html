About me

After almost a decade as a Maths teacher, I decided to look for a new path as a Software Engineer. I began with self-study using online resources, then joined General Assembly’s Software Engineer Immersive course. I have experience working in an intense environment where the expectations to deliver results according to deadlines are high. My international experience taught me how to work with people from different backgrounds, independently and as a team.
I see continuous learning as a way of life and enjoy studying new technologies and working on complex problems until I solve them. Ultimately, I love building and creating, so software engineering provided me with a great set of tools to do so. I am looking for a new challenge, one where I have the responsibility and the ability to have a meaningful impact while also continuing my own development as a Software Engineer.
